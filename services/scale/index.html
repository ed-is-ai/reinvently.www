The problem

Most AI projects that fail in production do not fail because the model was wrong. They fail because the surrounding infrastructure was not built for production conditions: no monitoring, no fallback behaviour, inadequate access controls, prompts that break under edge-case inputs, and no clear ownership of what happens when something goes wrong.

Going live without solving these problems first is not a calculated risk. It is a predictable incident waiting for a date.

What we do

We take your AI system from pilot-quality to production-ready. That means hardening the architecture, implementing observability and evaluation pipelines, building the access control and data governance framework, documenting operational procedures, and ensuring the people who will run this system know how to maintain it.

We work with your engineering, security, and compliance teams — this is a cross-functional engagement. The output is not a finished product handed over the fence. It is a system your team understands, can operate, and can continue developing.

What you get

  • Production-ready system — hardened architecture with monitoring, evaluation pipelines, fallback behaviour, and security controls appropriate to your risk profile
  • Governance framework — policies, access controls, audit logging, and accountability structures that meet your regulatory and organisational requirements
  • Operational runbook — documented procedures for operation, incident response, model updates, and ongoing evaluation — everything your team needs to run this independently
