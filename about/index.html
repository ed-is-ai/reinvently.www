About this blog

Curated analysis and research on enterprise AI adoption, with a UK focus.

Reinvently exists as a resource to help organisations adopting AI get the most relevant curated news. We publish curated technology analysis and AI research, with particular attention to the UK — its regulators, its public programmes, and the practical constraints its organisations work under.

The commitment is to practical advice grounded in first-hand experience. Where we compare tools, we have run them. Where we cite a statistic, we link the source. Where something is unproven or oversold, we say so. There is enough AI hype in circulation already; this blog is not adding to it.

What we cover

News tracks model releases, UK AI policy and regulation, and enterprise adoption trends — with analysis of what each development means in practice, not just what was announced.

Guides are in-depth technical pieces for practitioners and enterprise teams: tool comparisons, architecture deep dives, and how-tos drawn from real deployments.
